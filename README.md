# Instagram Content Performance Analysis
**Analyst:** Fatima Hassan  
**Tool:** Microsoft Excel  
**Date:** June 2026  

## Overview
Independent analysis of 30 Instagram posts (@fatiimaahh_, ~20k followers) 
spanning November 2025 to June 2026. The project identifies which content 
categories and post formats drive reach, engagement, and follower growth.

## Research Question
What content strategies maximise reach, engagement, and follower growth 
for a Pakistani-diaspora lifestyle creator on Instagram?

## Key Findings
- Eid content drove ~50% of total reach despite being a small % of posts
- Reels generated 6x more reach than Carousels (476k vs 73k)
- Carousels achieved higher engagement rate (11.6% vs 8.4%)
- Lifestyle and Eid content drove the most new followers (745 and 638)
